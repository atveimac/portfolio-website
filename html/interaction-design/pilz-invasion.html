The Mushroom Invasion!

Interaction Design | 2022

Interactive mario-themed game - this project combines the physical and digital realms, blending the charm of mario's world with interactive technology to create an engaging and fun experience for players.

The game begins when the player presses the start button. Subsequently, the required distance the player needs to achieve is displayed. This is followed by a 5-second timer during which the player must move the analog character within the track. Afterward, the display shows how close they were to the original value, whether they were spot-on, very close, or didn't make it at all.
